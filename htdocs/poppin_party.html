BACKGROUND

Poppin' Party, or Popipa, is one of the original 5 main bands of the BanG Dream! Franchise as well as one of the 4 live bands. The band consists of in-universe members Toyama Kasumi (Vocalist & Rhythm Guitarist), Hanazono Tae (Lead Guitarist), Ushigome Rimi (Bassist), Yamabuki Saaya (Drummer), and Ichigaya Arisa (Keyboardist). They are all from Hanasakigawa Girls' High School and are all 2nd years. They usually rehearse at the basement of Arisa's house. Though their experience and raw skill is not the best, they make up for it with their strong connection and energy they have during their performances.
The original franchise project was called "BanG_Dream!" in 2015 with the original members being Terakawa Aimi (VA: Kasumi), Nishimoto Rimi (VA: Rimi), and Itou Ayasa (VA: Arisa). The whole premise of it was for voice actresses to play instruments and perform live with the Idolmaster (or IM@S) franchise as the inspiration for the concept since Aimi was affiliated with it, and was able to sing and play the guitar. The other two members, Otsuka Sae (VA: Tae) and Ohashi Ayaka (VA: Saaya) became members of the band on BanG_Dream!'s 3rd and 4th live respectively.
The story of the in-universe band follows Kasumi who was always searching for the "Star Beat", something sparkling and heart-pounding, which she refers to as "Kirakira Dokidoki". She found her guitar, the "Random Star", from a place called Ryuuseidou, a pawnshop that Arisa's grandparents run. With this, Arisa led her to the SPACE Live House where they would watch performances together. Kasumi thought that this was the "sparkling, heart-pounding" thing she was looking for. This inspiration, along with her eagerness to practice and play the guitar, led her to the idea of forming a band with Arisa, Rimi, and Saaya, and later on Tae.
In-game, Popipa has 4 band stories (Band Story 0-3), titled as "Poppin'Party is Born!", "Colorful☆Poppin’Candy", "Double Rainbow", and "Live Beyond", with the latter 2 are to be added in EN servers soon. All band stories add up to 77 chapters.

 

MEDIA

Poppin' Party was featured in all 3 seasons of the anime, being the main protagonists of the 1st season, which is also their Band Story 0 in Girls Band Party. Their songs were used as opening and ending themes for the majority of the anime. They were also featured in all 3 seasons of GARUPA☆PICO as Kasumi, along with the other vocalists, was one of the singers for the theme songs for each season.

As one of the original 5 bands, they were also featured in "BanG Dream! FILM LIVE" and "BanG Dream! FILM LIVE 2nd Stage", the latter being joined by RAISE A SUILEN and Morfonica.

Popipa has their own film called "BanG Dream! Poppin'Dream" produced by Bushiroad and by SANZIGEN Animation Studio. The film premiered on January 1, 2022 in Japanese cinemas. It takes place after the 3rd season of the anime focusing on only Poppin' Party where their next stage seems to take place overseas.

 

MEMBERS / CHARACTERS

Toyama Kasumi (戸山 香澄)

Toyama Kasumi is the main protagonist of the franchise, as well as the leader, songwriter, vocalist, and rhythm guitarist of Poppin' Party. She has a bubbly personality, is always playful, active, optimistic, and extraverted in general. She interacts a lot with the other bands, considering how lively and friendly she is.

She is classmates with Arisa. Her epithet is "Starry Charisma" as she has a hobby of collecting star-shaped objects.
Class: 2-E
Birthday: December 4
Height: 164cm

Hanazono Tae (花園 たえ)

Hanazono Tae is the lead guitarist of Poppin' Party. She has been playing guitar since elementary and had a part-time job at SPACE. She is also a rabbit-lover and has a great understanding of music, assumed to have perfect pitch. Though she is known to be airheaded, she is pretty optimistic and relaxed, along with a calm sense of mind.

Her epithet is "Chasing Rabbits in the Garden".
Class: 2-B
Birthday: March 23
Height: 150cm

Ushigome Rimi (牛込 りみ)

Ushigome Rimi is the bassist and composer of Poppin' Party. She is generally shy and reserved, who wishes she could become more confident. She was from the Kansai region and sometimes unintentionally blends Kansai dialect in her speech. She has an obsession with chocolate cornets from the Yamabuki Bakery.

She is classmates with Saaya. Her epithet is "Cornet at First Sight".
Class: 2-B
Birthday: May 19
Height: 157cm

Yamabuki Saaya (山吹 沙綾)

Yamabuki Saaya is the drummer of Poppin' Party. She is the eldest of three children and works at her family bakery, the "Yamabuki Bakery". She was part of a band called "CHiSPA" before and quit, only regaining her passion as a drummer because of Kasumi. She is an easygoing, hardworking, and kindhearted girl who cherishes her loved ones. She is regarded as the "older sister" or "mother" of the band.

She is classmates with Rimi. Her epithet is the "Fermentation Girl".
Class: 2-A
Birthday: October 27
Height: 152cm

Ichigaya Arisa (市ヶ谷 有咲)

Ichigaya Arisa is the keyboardist of Poppin' Party. She played piano before but quit due to losing motivation and interest, however she picked it up again when she was invited by Kasumi to form a band. Her personality is more on the introverted side, being quite moody and hot-headed, though she is refined, polite, and softhearted. The term "tsundere" can best describe her.

She is classmates with Kasumi and is the student council secretary of Hanasakigawa. Her epithet is "Sweet and Spicy Personality".

Terakawa Aimi (寺川 愛美)

Terakawa Aimi, simply Aimi or Aimin, voices Toyama Kasumi and is the vocalist and rhythm guitarist of Poppin' Party. She was born on December 25, 1991. She dreamed of becoming a singer since she was young, and participated in singing contests and seiyuu auditions during the late 2000s. She plays the saxophone, studies languages, and collects figures. Her performing and playing the guitar during an Idomaster live in 2014 was the whole inspiration of the franchise.

She is a seiyuu affiliated with HiBiKi while her musical activities are under King Records.
Nickname: Saechi

Otsuka Sae (大塚 紗英)

Otsuka Sae, or Saechi, voices Hanazono Tae and is the lead guitarist of Poppin' Party. Her birth date is on October 10, 1995. She was in the brass band during middle school and became a street performer playing acoustic guitar when she was 15. She writes music, reads manga, and plays the piano and trombone. Classical music seems to be her favorite genre. She is also fond of rabbits similar to Tae, who used to own 23 of them.

She is associated with Ace Crew Entertainment.
Nickname: Rimi-rin

Nishimoto Rimi (西本 りみ)

Nishimoto Rimi, or Rimi-rin, voices Ushigome Rimi and is the bassist of Poppin' Party. She was born on October 25, 1994. She wanted to become a voice actor since she was a child and she did, debuting in 2016. She is a talented musician, knowing how to play the piano, guitar, bass, electone, and euphonium. Her hobbies are watching anime, movies, and dramas, as well as cosplaying.

She is also affiliated with HiBiKi.
Nickname: Hasshi

Ohashi Ayaka (大橋 彩香)

Ohashi Ayaka, or Hasshi, voices Yamabuki Saaya and is the drummer of Poppin' Party. Her birth date is on September 13, 1994. She was a child actress, debuted as a seiyuu in 2012 and as a singer in 2014. Her hobbies are watching anime, drumming, gaming, watching sports, reading light novels, and karaoke. She loves the genres of rock and heavy metal.

She is a seiyuu under Hiropro while her solo singing activities are signed under Lantis Records.
Nickname: Aya-chan

Itou Ayasa (伊藤 彩沙)

Itou Ayasa, called Aya-chan or simply Ayasa, voices Ichigaya Arisa and is the keyboardist/pianist for Poppin' Party. She was born on August 17, 1996. She used to be the president of her Theater Club in high school and was part of a choir. She debuted as a seiyuu in 2013 forming the idol unit "Milky Holmes Feahers" along with Aimi. Her hobbies include watching cooking shows, idols, theater plays, collecting cosmetics, and playing games.

She is also affiliated with HiBiKi.
