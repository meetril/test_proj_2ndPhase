BACKGROUND

RAISE A SUILEN (abbreviated as RAS) is one of the main bands of the BanG Dream! Franchise and is also one of the 4 live bands. The band consists of members Wakana Rei (Vocalist/Bassist), Asahi Rokka (Guitarist), Satou Masuki (Drummer), Nyubara Reo (Keyboardist), and Tamade Chiyu (DJ). The band goes by stage names: LAYER, LOCK, MASKING, PAREO, AND CHU2 respectively.
Originally having 4 members and called THE THIRD (仮), the group was a backup band for the non-live bands Afterglow, Pastel*Palettes, and Hello, Happy World! for the seiyuus were unable to perform their characters' respective instruments while the vocalists were given a chance to perform. On their 2nd live on July 2018, they announced their official band name "RAISE A SUILEN" with the addition of Tsumugi Risa as the band's DJ.
The band was officially added to BanG Dream! Girls Band Party on June 10, 2020 for JP servers and on June 11, 2021 for EN servers. They currently have 2 band stories in the JP server titled "RAISE A SUILEN -Raise the Curtain-" and "CORUSCATE-DNA-" which sum up to a total of 41 chapters.

 

MEDIA

RAISE A SUILEN was featured in the BanG Dream! 2nd Season, acting as a cameo band which was part of some scenes and performances. In the 3rd season, the band had their own part of the story which were adapted into events in the game.

Similar to the anime, RAS's in-universe characters were featured in some episodes of the 2nd season of GARUPA☆PICO known as "BanG Dream! GARUPA☆PICO ~Ohmori~", however they were only actually added recently in the 3rd season "BanG Dream! GARUPA☆PICO Fever!", along with Morfonica. LAYER was one of the singers of the theme song of the 3rd season, along with the vocalists of the other bands.

The band, along with the original 5 bands and Morfonica, was also featured in "BanG Dream! FILM LIVE 2nd Stage".

She is a 2nd year high school student at Geijutsu Academy's Musical Department. She is childhood friends with Hanazono Tae from Poppin' Party. Her epithet is "Respectfully Addressed".

Asahi Rokka aka LOCK (朝日 六花 / ロック)

Asahi Rokka (Stage Name: LOCK) is the guitarist of RAS. Rokka is kind and hardworking with a tendency to be shy. She is also supportive and courageous, a girl who worked hard in order to afford her guitar.

She is a 1st year high school student at Haneoka Girls' High School. She has part-time jobs at Live House Galaxy and the Asahi Bathhouse, a bathhouse owned by her relatives where she also stays. Her epithet is the "Guitar Berserker".

Satou Masuki aka MASKING (佐藤 ますき / マスキング)

Satou Masuki (Stage Name: MASKING) is the drummer for RAS. She is enthusiastic and is also a caring member of the band. She started playing the drums due to her yearning to become a drummer similar to her father.

She is a 2nd year high school student at Shirayuki Private Academy and has a part-time job at Ramen Ginga. Her epithet is "Stray Drummer: Passion".

Nyubara Reona aka PAREO (鳰原 令王那 / パレオ)

Nyubara Reona (Stage Name: PAREO) is the keyboardist of RAS. She admires CHU2 deeply, being the first one to be scouted by her. With the band, she is very lively and energetic, but is seen as serious and quiet in school.

She is a 2nd year middle school student at Kamogawa Chuou Middle School. She is a hardcore fan of Pastel*Palettes. Her epithet is "Faithful Dog Pareko".

Tamade Chiyu aka CHU2 (珠手 ちゆ / チュチュ)

Tamade Chiyu (Stage Name: CHU2) is the leader, producer, and DJ of RAS. As the leader of the band, she is professional, competitive, and also stubborn. She is seen as arrogant towards other bands and would even raise her voice at her own band members sometimes.

She is a 2nd year high school student at Celosia Girls' Academy. She sees Yukina (and Roselia as a whole) as her rival. Her epithet is the "Ankle-biting Child of the Revolution".

Raychell (レイチェル)

Raychell, or Rei/Cheru, is the vocalist and bassist of RAS being the voice of Wakana Rei and is also referred to as the "mother" of the band by other RAS members. She was born on January 13, 1985. She debuted as a solo singer in 2010. In 2017, she became one of the three new members of the band "SHAZNA" as the vocalist.

She is affiliated with Rhythm Zone while her solo singing career and activities are under Ace Crew Entertainment.

Kohara Riko (小原 莉子)

Kohara Riko, or Rikopin, voices Asahi Rokka and is the guitarist of RAS. Her birthday is on February 3, 1990. She started playing guitar when she was 18 and debuted as a voice actress in 2014. Her hobbies include bungee jumping, aromatherapy, cosplaying, and cooking.

She is a seiyuu under Object Inc.

Natsume (夏芽)

Matsuura Natsume, called as NatsuP or Nacchan, is the drummer of RAS and voices Satou Masuki. She was born on July 20, 1986. Along with Raychell, she was also one of the three new members of "SHAZNA" in 2017 taking the role as a dummer. She played xylophone in middle school and played drums when she joined her high school's light music club. Her hobbies are puzzle games and watching movies.

She is signed under Ace Crew Entertainment.

Kurachi Reo (倉知 玲鳳)

Kurachi Reo, or Reochi, is the keyboardist of RAS who also voices Nyubara Reona. She was born on May 22, 1997, being the band's youngest member. She has been playing piano since she was 2 years old. She debuted as a voice actress in 2017 and became a member of RAS in 2018. Her skills are playing piano, dancing, and calligraphy. She was also classmates with Maeshima Ami (Pastel*Palettes - Vo.) and were both part of the Marching Band Club in high school.

She is affiliated with S Inc.
Nickname: TsumuTsumu
Age: 25

Tsumugi Risa (紡木 吏佐)

Tsumugi Risa, or TsumuTsumu, voices Tamade Chiyu and is the DJ of RAS. She was born on July 5, 1996. She was scouted and became a seiyuu in 2017 and her addition to RAS was announced during THE THIRD's 2nd Live. She has a hyper and energetic personality. Her hobbies are travelling, drawing, and playing tennis. She is also skilled in the English language.

She is affiliated with HiBiKi.
